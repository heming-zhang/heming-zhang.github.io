About

Now, I am a second-year PhD candidate in Washington University School of Medicine(WUSM). My research interests span machine learning and precision medicine.

In 2021, my scholarly journey culminated in the successful procurement of a Master of Science in Computer Science from Washington University. During my master's study, I joined Dr. Fuhai Li's Lab as a research assistant in Institute for Informatics, Washington University School of Medicine in St. Louis to use computational and deep learning models, especially graph neural networks, to analyse cells signaling interactions and predict drug combinations effect.

And I finished my Bachelor degree in Information Systems in 2019 as "Boya" Plan Student and won the honor of "Outstanding Graduate" from Central China Normal University(CCNU), where I mainly carried out researches on text mining.

In 2018, I obtained the fellowship from Mitacs to become a VIRS in UBC with helpful supervision of Dr. Ivan Beschastnikh to carry on research about system machine learning.
